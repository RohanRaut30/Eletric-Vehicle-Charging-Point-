About Us


Mission

“EV Motors India has been set-up to provide “Energy as a Service” solution to e-mobility consumers by leveraging EV Charging & Energy Storage infrastructure developed on our unique cutting edge technology platforms.


About RRTek

“RRTek is a brand established by EV Motors India Pvt.Ltd. for the development of Public Charging Network in India. It seeks to establish a network of Public Charging Infrastructure through partnership with Real Estate Developers, Electric Vehicle OEMs, Charger Manufacturer, Service Centres and support from the financial Institutions and various state DISCOMS. Through these channel partners RRTek plans to achieve growth and scalability in Indian e-mobility system”


RRTek has a vision to leverage some of these partnerships to expand its network not only for Indian market but for global market as well. Our strategic association with global manufacturers allows RRTek to look beyond India to support global EV owners.
